= Beyond the Basics
include::_attributes.adoc[]


This guide provides xref:ug:btb:ugbtb.adoc#other-techniques[more advanced] guidance on writing maintainable larger
applications.

Later chapters discuss how to xref:ug:btb:ugbtb.adoc#deployment[deploy] your app, and discuss other ways in which you can xref:ug:btb:ugbtb.adoc#[extend] or adapt the framework itself to your particular needs.
